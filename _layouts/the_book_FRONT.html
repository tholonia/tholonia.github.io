THOLONIA: The Mechanics of Existential Awareness

This is the original book on the tholonic model, which was essentially a collection of notes, theories, explanations, and examples, some of which were fringe and some of which were quite mainstream. Some of the ideas in this book have since been discarded, while others have been significantly further developed. This book is currently no longer available as a published work, but it is made available here for historical reference and for those who really enjoy going down rabbit holes.
